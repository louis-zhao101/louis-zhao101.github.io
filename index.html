Earnings Reaction Model

Python XGBoost ML Finance

A machine learning pipeline for predicting post-earnings stock price movements, built on 15,000+ S&P 500 earnings events and a focused study of 35 AI Infrastructure companies. Combines FMP price & earnings data, SEC EDGAR 8-K press releases parsed by Claude AI (extracting guidance tone, management confidence, and AI-demand signals), and FRED macro indicators. Includes two comparative analyses: a broad S&P 500 statistical baseline and a sector-specific study of how the AI boom has changed earnings reaction dynamics.
